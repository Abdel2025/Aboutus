Who We Are

Founded in 2020, Morocco USA Education is an educational consulting centre
engaged in international education, training, and development activities in
the Middle East and North Africa. We believe that self-forged, fulfilled lives
lead to understanding, mutual respect, and a more peaceful, productive world.

Our Vision

is a world where all people have access to opportunities that enable them to go as far as their dreams, talents, and dedication can take them.
